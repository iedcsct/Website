IEDC SCTCE

Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Cell

Our Vision


To develop a knowledge ecosystem with required infrastructure that can enable students and faculty to innovate and prototype their potential ideas with support from government, industry and reputed academic institutions around the world and help them to realize their potentials.

About IEDC


IEDC SCTCE is aimed at direct involvement of the student community in the creation of a sustainable entrepreneurial ecosystem in our college. IEDC SCTCE will be a hub of activities of entrepreneurship development activities in our college under the IEDC program of the Kerala Startup Mission. We focus the promotion of Innovation (Entrepreneurship through Innovation) and that should lead to a startup. Basically it create a product development ecosystem in our college.

Mission


  • Providing trainings and workshops in ideation, skill development and entrepreneurial awareness

  • Developing technical communities in the campus.

  • Encouraging the students and faculty members to carry out innovative research work.
  • Creating an ecosystem where makers, innovators and entrepreneurs can co-exist and contribute growth.

SWALE

Journalism can never be silent: that is its greatest virtue and its biggest fault. It must speak, and speak instantly, while the echoes of wonder, the claims of triumph, and the signs of horror are still in the air. SWA LE was a news reporting event conducted by IEDC SCTCE to discover hidden journalists. The event was held on November 1, 2020, and it had two phases where the initial session was news reporting while the next round was news reading.IEDC never goes wrong to reveal the talent in people and the best were chosen as winners.

MANOMI

A woman with a voice is, by definition, a strong woman. But the search to find that voice can be remarkably difficult. The IEDC SCTCE conducted a one-hour story writing competition named 'Manomi' on 8th March 2019 as a part of the International Women's Day celebration. It was the first-ever open space competition in the history of SCTCE and was conducted near the cafeteria.

BRAINZ ABLAZE

“Brain Ablaze” was the first event conducted by IEDC in the lockdown period. It was an online intercollegiate quiz competition focusing on general knowledge, logical reasoning, and entrepreneurship skills. The registration was free of cost and the enrolled students were to contest individually and not as a team. 20 questions were given out as a Google Form to the contestants, which were to be answered and turned in within the given time.

INNOVATION CHALLENGE

Innovation is the unrelenting drive to break the status quo and develop a new where few have dared to go. With this objective in mind, we conducted Innovation Challenge, an ideation competition. It was a huge success where we had two phases -the prelims round and the final round. It was fascinating enough for the participants.

AGORA

The program included some informative and interactive talks to give them a basic idea about IEDC, its ideals, and objectives and a purely interactive session engaging our students with entrepreneurship and marketing skills development workshop helping them to develop more creative ideas and a good team spirit. The students were highly enthusiastic and talented that they were able to bring out many ideas that could be of value, in this competitive world.

FUNDAMENTAL OF ARDUINO

Fundamentals of Arduino was a online workshop conducted by IEDC SCTCE. Here our cell mainly focuses to improve the Arduino coding skills of the participants and it was conducted through an online platform called Tinkercad. The session was taken by Mr. Vasudev, 4th-year Computer Science student. Nearly 100 students attended the workshop session.

ETINCELLE

Etincelle is an intercollegiate managerial event conducted by IEDC SCTCE in collaboration with IEDC LBS. It is an event which consist of five rounds namely aptitude round, 1 minute elevator pitch along with bplan building, crisis solving event, a group discussion and a pressure round, after which the top 3 contestants will be recruited as interns at Accubits, an AI and blockchain focused solutions company

COLOURS OF FREEDOM

This is a Digital Poster designing competition conducted by IEDC SCTCE. This event had many participants and they were given a topic: 'FREEDOM IN MODERN INDIA', which brought out the essence of artist inside everyone.

MIXED UP

Mixed Up was a fun event conducted by IEDC SCTCE, the clues and scramble will be provided as story in IEDC SCTCE Instagram page. The participants should DM the screenshot of the completed scramble.

THE TRAIL

The Trail was an inter-college online Treasure Hunt contest that spanned over three days onward with the thrill and enthusiasm of the participants. The task of every crew was to tackle 10 challenges in the quickest duration of time. It seized the participants for a ride to the realm of puzzles through the questions and hints provided

THOUGHT PROVOKE SHARE

The happiness of our life relies on the quality of our thoughts. As a part of the 88th birth anniversary of former President Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, 'Thought - Provoke - Share,' an open discussion platform was performed. A brief synopsis of Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam’s ideals and visions, student's unique interests, and perspectives on numerous themes like space technology movies and their significance on society has been communicated.

HELLO MR. MANAGER

People always appreciate and look upon those who have good marketing skills. Hello. Mr manager an event directed to enhance the leadership quality and marketing skills of participants. It discovered the competitor with their best managerial skills

SLIDES AWAY

The objective of a presentation is not only to convey knowledge but to renovate the audience. Slides Away, a slideshow/presentation creating competition was conducted to pull out innovations for representing ideas. The event stood as an initiative to facilitate imaginative digital marketing strategies among youthful students.

GAME DEVELOPMENT WITH UNITY

Every once in a while, a new technology, an old problem, and a big idea turn into an innovation. The main objective of the webinar was to upgrade and update students on game development with unity. It was actually an usher in for those who look upon game development. The students had a good interactive and learning session with the Expert.
